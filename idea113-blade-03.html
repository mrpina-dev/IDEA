01 — Hex Shaft Bore
The hex shaft is 0.500 in / 12.700 mm nominal (1/2" aluminum hex). Components that the shaft passes through must have a bore that allows assembly without forcing but does not allow wobble. Clearance fit target bore: 0.516 in / 13.106 mm. Acceptable range: 0.508–0.524 in / 12.90–13.31 mm.
Target: 0.516" / 13.106mm  |  Range: 0.508"–0.524" / 12.90–13.31mm
Awaiting input
02 — Press Fits (Printed-to-Printed)
Press fits between 3D printed ABS parts require the shaft/pin to be 0.006 in / 0.15 mm smaller than the bore (interference fit). Example: a 0.500" pin presses into a 0.494" bore. Too much interference will crack printed parts. This check only applies if your assembly uses printed-to-printed press fits.
Target interference: 0.006" / 0.15mm (shaft = bore − 0.006")
0 / 1 sentence minimum
03 — Minimum Wall Thickness (ABS / 3D Printed)
Structural ABS walls must be minimum 2.0 mm / 0.079 in. Absolute floor is 1.2 mm / 0.047 in (2 perimeters at 0.6mm nozzle). Walls thinner than 2.0mm will flex under impact. Walls thinner than 1.2mm will not print reliably. This check only applies if your design includes 3D printed parts.
Structural min: 2.0mm / 0.079"  |  Absolute floor: 1.2mm / 0.047"
Awaiting input
04 — Minimum Feature Size (3D Print, 0.6mm Nozzle)
At a 0.6mm nozzle, the minimum printable feature is 1.2 mm / 0.047 in (2 perimeters). Features smaller than this — thin fins, sharp tips, narrow slots — will either not print or will print poorly. Design around this constraint or accept that fine details will not resolve cleanly.
Minimum feature: 1.2mm / 0.047"
Awaiting input
05 — Minimum Metal Wall (Laser Cut Parts)
Laser cut metal parts should respect minimum wall thicknesses: Steel min 0.031 in / 0.8 mm, Aluminum min 0.039 in / 1.0 mm. Thinner walls may distort during cutting or deform under impact loads.
Steel min: 0.031" / 0.8mm  |  Aluminum min: 0.039" / 1.0mm
Awaiting input
06 — Hex Shaft Engagement Confirmation
Your Assignment 02 build plan specified a hex shaft engagement depth. Confirm your SolidWorks model reflects that planned depth. If it changed, document the deviation and reason.
Enter both values to compare
07 — Hex Extension Height
The hex shaft must extend above the full body height by 0.50 in ± 0.05 in (12.70 mm ± 1.27 mm). Acceptable range: 0.45–0.55 in / 11.43–13.97 mm. This extension mates with the standard launcher. Too short and the launcher won't grip. Too long and launch will be inconsistent.
Target: 0.500" / 12.70mm  |  Range: 0.45"–0.55" / 11.43–13.97mm
